# Kids Arcade Roadmap

## Ready Now (Short Builds)

- Color Match Pop: tap matching colored bubbles before time runs out.
- Balloon Defender: pop balloons before they reach the top.
- Fruit Catcher: catch falling fruit, avoid rotten items.
- Puzzle Slider: 3x3 or 4x4 tile puzzle with animals or icons.
- Snowball Toss: aim-and-throw target game with moving targets.

## Next Up (Medium Builds)

- Animal Memory Flip: classic memory cards with animals and sounds.
- Space Dodger: move a spaceship to dodge asteroids and collect stars.
- Jungle Jump: endless jumper with platforms and light obstacles.
- Rocket Launch: hold/release power meter to dodge obstacles.
- Traffic Hop: cross lanes safely with timing challenges.

## Stretch Goals (Larger Builds)

- Word Builder: drag letters to build simple words with hints.
- Maze Runner: tilt/arrow maze with multiple difficulty levels.
- Ocean Cleanup: collect trash while avoiding sea creatures.
- Piano Tiles: rhythm-based timing game with increasing speed.

## Common Features to Add Later

- Difficulty selector and kid-friendly pacing.
- Daily challenges and simple achievements.
- Local high scores stored in browser.
- Accessibility options: large buttons and reduced motion.
